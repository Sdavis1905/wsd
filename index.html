Naruto

Naruto is a teenage ninja from the Leaf Village who was ostracized as a child for being the jinchuriki of the Nine-tail Demon Fox, a malevolent creature that attacked the leaf village, which was sealed in his body as a newborn infant. As an outcast of the village, Naruto is determined in being recognized and becoming the hokage of the Leaf Village. As tremendous events fall throughout the story, he eventually becomes his dreams as a hero of the village.
