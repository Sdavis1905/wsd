Naruto

Naruto is a teenage ninja from the Leaf Village who was ostracized as a child for being the jinchuriki of the Nine-tail Demon Fox, a malevolent creature that attacked the leaf village, which was sealed in his body as a newborn infant. As an outcast of the village, Naruto is determined in being recognized and becoming the hokage of the Leaf Village. As tremendous events fall throughout the story, he eventually becomes his dream as a hero of the village.

Summary of Plot

Naruto goes through the first series of episodes in school learning to become a ninja. After the Chunin exams, his team member and conrad Sasuke Uchiha, decides to take his own path to achieve his ultimate goal of vengance. As Naruto fights to get his friend back, he faces many unfortunate events in the Shippuden series. His increase in strength is fueled by his will to retrieve his friend from the deep dark path he has taken.
